The Trench Mage

by J.C. Trent

To be seen is to be known. To be known is to invite obliteration.

On the Brynnland Front, they call Charlie-Three the Immortals — a platoon that walks out of battles that should have ground them to nothing. The army credits luck and good sergeants. The truth is Maren Vosk.

Trained to deflect bullets and break minds without a sound, Maren is a weapon disguised as a soldier. His job: protect his platoon. His rule: never let them find out. Because a mage who is known becomes a target — and every man standing near him becomes collateral.
