It's alive!

January 01, 2026

The site is live, which means I can finally point to a single place on the internet and say: yes, this is where the book stuff goes.

I’m J.C. Trent, an indie author currently preparing my debut novel for release. This site exists to track that process: what I’m writing, where each project stands, and what’s coming next.

What is this?

This is the obligatory author blog, except without the vacation updates or what I had for dinner. This site is focused entirely on my books and the process that takes them from a loose collection of ideas to something people can actually read.

If you’re looking for updates, project status, release plans, or format information, this is where it all lives. My goal is to keep the important details in one place so nobody has to do Discord archaeology or fight Reddit’s search function just to figure out how many books are planned in a series or how long I usually take between releases.

I don’t expect to post here very often—probably no more than once a month—but when I do, it should be useful.

Thanks for reading, and take a look at the Books page if you want to see what I’m working on.

Grab the template on GitHub
