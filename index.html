Void-Born · Book 1

Leviathan

by J.C. Trent

Vale Korin is void-born, raised in zero gravity, and her bones are failing.

Human biology was never meant for micro-g development. If Vale doesn’t reach Tier 2 by thirty, her skeleton will collapse and she’ll never walk again.

So she builds a reputation.

She rigs her own gear from salvaged drone parts. She takes the hardest jobs. She becomes the best void specialist in the system — the kind of operator who can beat claim jumpers to a derelict and survive six-on-one odds in hard vacuum.

Reputation cuts both ways.

When Vale draws the attention of the wrong people, she and the crew of the Rusty Lung are pulled into a salvage job no one has ever survived: two kilometers of dead ship, hard vacuum, and a pirate warlord convinced cultivation makes him untouchable.

The vacuum disagrees.

Oxygen doesn’t care about your tier. Strength means nothing without gravity to push against. On a derelict, the deadliest person isn’t the strongest.

It’s the one who knows the corridors.

The Void-Born is a science fiction progression novel with realistic zero-g physics, structured advancement, and found-family stakes.
